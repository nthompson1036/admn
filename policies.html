Return Policies

If you are not 100% satisfied with your purchase, you can return it! DHP will accept returns within 60 days of your in-store purchase or from the initial shipment date for online purchases.

In-Store

Your local Durham House of Pets store with accept the return of your DHP order within 60 days of the initial purchase or shipment date. Please be prepared to show either a Store Return Receipt or a Purchase Confirmation Email. Returns made without a receipt or made after 60 days are eligible for in-store only merchandise credit equal to the amount at which the returned products were sold.

Ship Items Back

Durham House of Pets orders can also be returned by mail within 60 days to one of our warehouses. A refund for the item will be issued once it has been received.
