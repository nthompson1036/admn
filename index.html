History

Durham House of Pets (DHP) was founded in 1980 by two brothers, Drake and Josh. Drake and Josh grew up on a farm, which had been passed down through their family for 60 years! Undoubtedly, the love of animals has always been a staple in their family history. As the brothers got older, they wanted to truly embrace their family values and open up a pet store to provide supplies and accessories for everyones favorite furry friend! The first corporate office was opened in 1980 in Central Valley, California.

They initially only sold food for cats and dogs, but as pets became more popular members of families, they expanded the products they offered in stores. They started incorporating toys for different animals, and also clothes and accessories. As demand for their products increased, they expanded into different stores around the country. They opened a regional office in Chicago in 1985, followed by another office in Boston in 1990. Expanding their locations enabled anyone across the country to have access to the best pet products.

Why Use Us

Durham House of Pets is well-known for its reputation of providing the best brands for pets and also the pet-friendly people that are hired. Our company stays up-to-date on all pet brands and ensures that the products sold are the best of the best and will keep your pets healthy and happy! Our team does extensive research on products to make sure that there are no additives or other ingredients that are harmful to pets. A healthy pet makes for a happy family and we want to make sure you are confident in the food you feed your pet.

The staff we hire goes through a training once a month to ensure they are up-to-date on the latest products and trends in the pet industry and also for pet care. We want to make sure our staff is well-versed on the care for different kinds of pets, so that they are able to make the best recommendations to our customers! We also encourage those on our staff who have pets to try out our products so they can even give personal recommendations based on what their pets liked the best!

We believe that pets are an essential part of the family and want them to feel just as loved and cared for as anyone else!
